⚠️ Bulging

A bulging disc occurs when the outer fibrous ring (annulus fibrosus) weakens and extends beyond the normal disc boundary without rupturing. This may cause localised back pain or mild nerve compression.

🔴 Herniation

Disc herniation occurs when the nucleus pulposus protrudes through a tear in the annulus fibrosus. This can compress adjacent nerve roots, resulting in radiculopathy, sciatica, or motor/sensory deficits.
